Admission Process

At Vishal Bharti School, we strive to make the admission process as smooth and transparent as possible. Our goal is to welcome students who are eager to learn and grow in a supportive environment.

Steps for Admission:

  • Application Form: Begin by completing the admission form, available online or at the school office.
  • Document Submission: Submit required documents such as birth certificate, previous school records, and passport-sized photographs.
  • Entrance Assessment: Depending on the grade level, an entrance test or assessment may be conducted to understand the student's academic strengths and areas for development.
